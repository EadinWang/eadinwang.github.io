Background

This is a project done by Robin Xie and me, under course collaboration between "Digital Sculpture" and "Emmersive Arts". I'm responsive for the physical sculpture part. 

*Because of the 3 months city-wide lockdown in Shanghai, sculpture was only implemented by 3D model animation. :(

Overview

We chose a relatively brave and broad concept for this project. Initially we started from the zen aesthetics and traditional Chinese philosophy that examines the relationship between our inner heart and the outside world. Later, developing on the interrelationship of inner and outer world, we focused on how to represent and visualize the flow in-between, and the ultimate key element is water.
